Fabrication

A case study for testing GA's on soft robotic actuators.

The initial prototype will be additively manufactured for comparison to the final model that is produced.

Future Testing Plan and Validation

  • After manufacturing this prototype, there will be an experimental set up for comparsion of simulation results.
  • Then for the angular displacement, a board of angular displacement will be shown in comparsion from initial to final flexural displacement of the system.
  • In terms of testing input pressure, there will be a prototype to test for failure to state the maximum pressure that the resulting component would fail at.
