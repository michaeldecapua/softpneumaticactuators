Fitness Function

A case study for testing GA's on soft robotic actuators.

Fitness Function

Variable 1: Angular Displacement

  • This actuator has the application of a soft robotic gripper, therefore this objective is to maximize the bend of the device.

Variable 2: Input Pressure

  • The soft robotic actuator will be inflated to a certain level of pressure for inflating the air chambers within the device.
  • Since the intake pressure is delivered from an external device, the objective is to minimize the intake pressure required.
